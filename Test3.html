Personal Logo

The first media work I was introduced to was a logo design.

The image I wanted to create was [a light that shines in the dark]. I've always loved how a light is brightest and most valuable in the dark. I chose to draw the moon - a light that doesn't faint even through many nights. Also, the moon's shadow is to express the other side I had experienced as a young immigrant.

In addition, I engraved the Korean characters of my first name, ㄷ and ㅎ. I wanted to express my identity as a Korean American.
